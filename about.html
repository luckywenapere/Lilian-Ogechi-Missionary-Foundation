Our Story

"Missionary zeal does not grow out of intellectual beliefs, nor out of theological arguments, but out of love." ‚Äî Roland Allen

Drawing strength from the above renowned quote, hence the birth of Lilian Ogechi Missionary Foundation (LOMF), Umueleke, Ehime, Mbano, Imo State, in 2018 by Mrs. Lilian Ogechi Uche as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Birth of LOMF' was a call from God, and as a believer in Christ Jesus, who vehemently understands the biblical injunction that the spirit of Christ is the spirit of mission, and reaching out to the unsaved and underserved is a top priority.

As a missionary organisation, we have been involved in taking the gospel of Christ Jesus by way of our yearly medical outreaches in various towns and villages in Imo State and beyond. As a burgeoning organisation, with a vision to be missionary foundation that truly caters for the needs of the downtrodden in the society, the aged people, people who can't afford access to quality medical care, we will ensure that these gaps are filled-up and Jesus Christ introduced to them as the giver of good life. Though, encumbered by funding, we hope to expand our humanitarian (medical and evangelistic) activities to the unreached.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To share the love of Christ through evangelism and compassionate humanitarian service, providing medical care and support to underserved communities while bringing hope to the unreached.

Our Vision

To be a leading missionary foundation that transforms lives by spreading the gospel of Christ, promoting access to quality healthcare, and uplifting the downtrodden in Nigeria and beyond.
