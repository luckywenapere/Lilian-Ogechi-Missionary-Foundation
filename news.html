Successful Medical Outreach in Enugu

Oct 15, 2025 • Health

Enugu, Nigeria — On October 15, our volunteers and partner clinics provided free medical consultations, essential medicines, and health education to more than 400 individuals in need. The outreach included general check-ups, maternal care advice, and distribution of medications. Local community leaders and health professionals collaborated to ensure services reached remote neighborhoods.

We want to thank every volunteer and partner for their time and generosity. The outreach also included a short training for community health volunteers to ensure continued care following our visit. Read our full impact summary and photos in the gallery.
